About Me

I am a PhD scholar in the Department of Civil Engineering at IIT Delhi with a strong academic background (M.Tech CGPA: 9.2) and hands-on experience in experimental and numerical modeling of reinforced and ultra-high-performance concrete. My research focuses on nonlinear behavior, moment-curvature relationships, and advanced materials testing techniques.

Research Interests

  • Reinforced and Ultra High Performance Concrete (UHPC)
  • Moment-Curvature Analysis using MATLAB
  • Finite Element Modeling (Abaqus)
  • Experimental Testing of Concrete Cubes and Cylinders
